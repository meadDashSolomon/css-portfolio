About Mead Solomon

Just the highlights

Howdy! I'm a Software Engineer. My organized and conscientious approach helps me thrive in back-end development. Yet, my true passion lies in front-end development, where I enjoy building beautiful and functional websites.

My journey into software engineering began during the pandemic when my law school transitioned to distance learning. This shift provided an opportunity to reevaluate my strengths and interests, leading me to explore new career paths.

Curiosity led me to try coding, driven by numerous ideas for software projects I wanted to create. I embarked on this journey with The Odin Project, an online course that surprisingly captivated me. Contrary to my expectations of tedium, I found joy in the design, language, and problem-solving aspects of coding. Since that revelation, coding has become an integral part of my life.

My favorite technologies include:

  • React
  • CSS
  • Three.js
